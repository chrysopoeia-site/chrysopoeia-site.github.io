About

Chrysopoeia is the alchemical process of transmuting lesser metals into gold. It is also a nice word. My reason for choosing it is more for the latter, but the concept is still cool.

Technical Stuff

This site was hand-coded and for better or worse, it shows. I used plain HTML, CSS, and Jekyll for Static Site Generation. Javascript is only used for playing music.

The only font used on this site (so far) is Mx437_IBM_PS-55_re from the Ultimate Oldschool PC Font Pack (see below).
